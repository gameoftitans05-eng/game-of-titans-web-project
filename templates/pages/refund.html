Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: April 11, 2026

This Refund & Cancellation Policy applies to all registrations and payments made on Game of Titans, operated by UrbanRootss.

1. No Refund Policy

All payments for registrations and services are strictly non-refundable, except in specific cases below.

2. Event Cancellation by Organizer

  • Eligible for full or partial refund
  • Refund to original payment method
  • Processing time: 7–10 working days

3. Event Rescheduling

  • Registration remains valid
  • No refunds in such cases

4. Participant Cancellation

  • No refunds will be issued
  • Non-transferable unless approved

5. Failed or Duplicate Transactions

  • Contact us if payment is deducted but not confirmed
  • Duplicate payments will be refunded after verification
  • Processing time: 7–10 working days

6. Payment Gateway Issues

  • We are not responsible for network failures
  • Delays caused by banks or gateways

7. Force Majeure

No refunds in case of uncontrollable events such as:

  • Natural disasters
  • Government restrictions
  • Pandemics or emergencies

8. Refund Processing

  • Refunds to original payment method only
  • Processing time: 7–10 business days
  • Transaction/platform fees may be deducted
