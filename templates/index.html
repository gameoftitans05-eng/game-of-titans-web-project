HOW IT WORKS

THE ARCHITECTURE OF COMPETITION

01

Entry & Allocation

Athletes enter a structured pool and are allocated into competitive teams under celebrity captains. Every athlete starts on equal footing — selection is purely performance-driven.

Performance Selection

Teams undergo structured challenges where only consistent performance ensures survival. Captains refine squads through progressive elimination.

03

Final Squad Showdown

Final squads are locked and pushed into high-stakes battles where only the elite survive to claim ultimate victory.
